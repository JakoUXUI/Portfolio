About me

UX/UI & Graphic Designer

I use my creativity and expertise to create timeless expressions and meaningful experiences for brands in a primarily digital world.

I am a Graphic Designer fascinated by UX/UI Design. After having worked for more than 12 years in the graphic design sector and with all the multidisciplinar visual experience I have acquired, I have decided to take a step forward and focus my professional career on the creation of digital products.
